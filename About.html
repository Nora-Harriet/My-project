About Us

Our Story, Values and Passion for Great Food

Our Mission

Welcome to Eleven Meals, where tradition meets sophistication to bring you eleven healthy and delicious meals! Our mission is simple: to serve authentic Ghanaian cuisine made with the freshest ingredients, crafted with love, and delivered straight to your table. Whether you're craving a comforting classic or an exciting new flavor, we promise an experience that celebrates our rich food heritage and that delights your taste buds.

Our History and Inspiration

Our journey began with a passion for Ghanaian food and a desire to share it with the world. Inspired by time-honored recipes passed down through generations, we carefully select each dish on our menu to bring you the best of local flavors. From street food favorites to hearty home-cooked meals, our kitchen tells a story of culture, tradition, and culinary excellence. We also understand that in fast-paced cities like Ghana, many people—especially workers—often skip breakfast due to their busy schedules. That’s why we designed our menu to provide a rich elevenses, lunch and supper experience, ensuring that even the busiest individuals can enjoy nutritious, satisfying meals without compromise.

Our Values & Uniqueness

At Eleven Meals, we believe food should be fresh, flavorful and fulfilling while keeping it purely natural. We source our ingredients directly from local farmers to ensure quality and sustainability. Our chefs stay true to traditional cooking methods while adding a modern touch to enhance your dining experience. More than just a restaurant, we are committed to eco-friendly practices that support both our customers and the environment.

Meet the Team

Behind every dish is a dedicated team of chefs, food lovers, and customer service experts who make it all possible. With a shared passion for great food and hospitality, we work together to bring to your doorsteps meals that not only taste amazing but also make you feel at home.

Convenience and Pre-Orders

At Eleven Meals , we value your time and convenience. That’s why we offer large and pre-order deals to hospitals, schools, business institutions, and personal customers who need a hassle-free meal solution. Whether you're ordering for a group or just yourself, we bring the food to you, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience. For those who prefer to pick up their meals, we have a few designated pickup points where you can easily grab your order if you live nearby.
