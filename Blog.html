Sustainable Practices: How We're Helping You and the Environment

We prioritize the use of organic ingrediants that are sourced directly from local farms that use the best sustainability practices to ensure quality crops. Our delivery motorcycles operate on 100% solar power. We also use eco-friendly paper bags as well as recyclable utensils to package our meals. Due to our drive to reduce food waste, leftovers are refrigerated and later donated to the needy. Our restaurant is not only committed to providing you good meals but we're also committed to eco-friendly practices that support both our customers and the environment.
